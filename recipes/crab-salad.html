Crab Salad

Home

Description

This crab salad recipe, featuring budget-friendly ingredients, is as easy as it gets.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound imitation crabmeat, flaked
  • ½ cup finely chopped celery, or more to taste
  • ½ cup reduced-fat ranch dressing
  • ⅓ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Gently mix crabmeat, celery, ranch, mayonnaise, sugar, and parsley in a salad bowl until thoroughly combined.

  3. Refrigerate, stirring occasionally, for 8 hours to overnight.

  4. Stir again just before serving. Enjoy!
